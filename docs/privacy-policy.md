# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

Aquarium Water Log is a local-first mobile app for recording aquarium water test readings.

## Data The App Stores

The app stores the information you enter, including tank names, tank notes, water test values, target ranges, water change notes, reminder preference, and test history.

This data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. Aquarium Water Log does not require an account and does not send your aquarium records to a server.

## Data Collection And Sharing

Aquarium Water Log does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, cloud databases, hosted APIs, or third-party tracking services.

## Notifications

If you enable test reminders, the app may ask for notification permission. Reminder notifications are scheduled and managed locally on your device. They are not sent from a remote server.

## CSV Export

The app lets you export your saved water test records as a CSV file. Exporting uses your device's share sheet. You choose where the exported file goes and which app receives it.

## Data Deletion

You can delete individual water test records in the app, delete all saved tests for a selected tank, or reset all locally stored app data from Settings. You can also remove all locally stored app data by uninstalling Aquarium Water Log or clearing the app's storage through Android system settings.

## Children's Privacy

Aquarium Water Log is a general utility app and is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated if the app's behavior changes. Any future version should continue to describe what data is stored, whether it leaves the device, and how users can manage it.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the app developer through the support contact listed on the Google Play Store listing.
